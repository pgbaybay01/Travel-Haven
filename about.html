About Travel Haven

Learn about our story, mission, and the team behind your unforgettable travel experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Travel Haven began with a simple mission: to create unforgettable travel experiences that transform lives. What started as a small operation with just two passionate travelers has grown into a trusted travel agency serving thousands of adventurers each year.

Our founders, Christian, Jesvi, Prince and Vhens, met while playing Billiards at the island of Siargao and bonded over their shared love for exploration and cultural immersion. They realized that many travelers were missing out on authentic experiences due to mass tourism, and decided to create a company that would focus on meaningful travel.

Today, Travel Haven operates in over 50 countries, offering carefully curated travel packages that combine popular attractions with off-the-beaten-path experiences.

Our Mission & Values

Authentic Experiences

We believe travel should be transformative. We design our trips to provide genuine cultural immersion and meaningful connections with local communities.

Responsible Tourism

We are committed to sustainable travel practices that respect local cultures and environments. We partner with eco-friendly accommodations and support local businesses.

Exceptional Service

From the moment you book until you return home, our dedicated team ensures every aspect of your journey exceeds expectations.
